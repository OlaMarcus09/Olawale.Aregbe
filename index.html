01 — About

I build things
that matter.

I'm Olawale Marcus Aregbe — a self-driven frontend developer and founder based in Nigeria. I care deeply about clean interfaces, fast performance, and products that actually solve problems.

My flagship build is Workspace Africa — a platform designed to connect African professionals with flexible, modern coworking spaces across the continent. I also founded Siro, a tax compliance SaaS built specifically for Nigerian SMEs navigating FIRS regulations.

Beyond products, I've shipped client work across legal tech, blockchain, and enterprise software — from GrayPatch Solicitors to an Aptos blockchain explorer. I write code the way I think: clear, deliberate, and without unnecessary noise.
